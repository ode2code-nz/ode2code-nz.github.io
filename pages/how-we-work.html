Free Consultation and Brief

The Brief is the starting point of the client engagement for a project. The purpose of the Brief stage is to ensure that everyone shares an understanding of the problem and are confident both a feasible product is possible, and a partnership can be formed.

The brief phase begins with a quick 20-minute analysis call. That determines whether there is an early indication of fit for the project. Once we’ve determined there might be a fit, we enter into a two-hour Brief workshop. After that, we go away and collate our findings, and we present that back – usually about a week later, in a one-hour Brief review meeting. That also shows us the length of time that we are going to Scope for. We will produce a Brief document with our findings that are free for the customer even if they don’t proceed with us.

At the end of the brief process we will know what the problem statement is, and how long it’s going to take to Scope that out. That’s when we form our first commercial engagement. Once the service agreement is signed, we’ll assign the next available team to Scope the project.

Scoping Your Product

Scoping is where we define the solution to the problem. It is like creating the blueprints before building the house. Investing time at the start of the project will significantly mitigate risks down the track.

We work with you to understand your existing processes, ongoing problems and new ideas and convert them into a workable solution document. Our architects choose the right technologies and design a solution for the long term.

We want feedback as early as possible in the development process so we work on User Interface design and prototypes before we start writing code. We put this in front of you so that you can trust we will meet your requirements and ensure an optimal experience for your users. We turn your complex business processes into simple experiences for your end user. Not only will your app look great, it will be a pleasure to use.

Development

In the development stage, we take the design and build functioning software. We develop software following Agile principles. We look to the minimum set of features required to get a working product and we build this first. We work with fixed time frame "sprints" (usually 2 weeks) with clear deliverables that you shape with us. We demonstrate the working software to you at the end of each sprint.

Project Management and team leadership: ode2code leaders maintain the vision for your system. They communicate the software plan and command the highest quality with a commitment to excellence.

Back End Development: Our back end developers live in C# and .NET Core to build maintainable, scalable, high-performance code for your software solution.

Front End Development: Our front end developers use HTML, CSS, Javascript and XAML to bring UI designs to life. Using modern frameworks, we deliver the user experience that users have come to expect.

Support

Following the successful delivery of software, we can provide you with training and we offer options for ongoing support and updates. You'll have us on hand to ask questions, raise issues and to work through new ideas for your software systems.
